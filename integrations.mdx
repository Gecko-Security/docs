### Also available

- **ClickUp** — **Settings > ClickUp**. Track remediation alongside your team's existing work.
- **Shortcut** — **Settings > Shortcut**. Route findings into Shortcut stories.
- **GitLab vulnerability export** — **Settings > GitLab vulnerabilities**. Surface findings in GitLab-native security dashboards.
