---
title: "Shortcut"
description: "Route Gecko findings into Shortcut stories."
---

Connect Shortcut to turn findings into stories for your team. Configure it in
**Settings** &gt; **Shortcut**.

## Connect

<Steps>
<Step title="Add a Shortcut API token">
  Unlike the OAuth-based trackers, Shortcut connects with an **API token**. Paste
  your token in **Settings** &gt; **Shortcut** and save.
</Step>
<Step title="Choose a group">
  Select the Shortcut **group** that should own the stories.
</Step>
<Step title="Enable auto-create">
  Turn on **auto-create** to file a Shortcut story for every finding after each
  scan.
</Step>
</Steps>
