---
title: "Linear"
description: "Push Gecko findings into your Linear backlog with the right priority."
---

Connect Linear so findings move into normal backlog ownership. Configure it in
**Settings** &gt; **Linear**.

## Connect

<Steps>
<Step title="Authorize Linear">
  Click **Connect** and complete the Linear OAuth flow.
</Step>
<Step title="Choose a team and project">
  Select the Linear **team** that should receive issues, and optionally a
  **project**.
</Step>
<Step title="Set defaults">
  Pick a **default assignee** and any **labels**. Optionally enable
  **priority mapping** so a finding's severity maps to a Linear priority.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Auto-create issues

Enable **auto-create** to file a Linear issue for every finding after each scan.
A team must be selected first.

<Tip>
  Use priority mapping so Critical and High findings land at the top of the
  backlog automatically.
</Tip>
