---
title: "Audit log"
description: "Review access and configuration events for your team."
---

The audit log records access and configuration events so you can answer who did
what, and when. Find it in **Settings** &gt; **Audit Log**.

<Note>
  The audit log is an Enterprise feature. Admins and Managers can view it; see
  [Teams & permissions](/teams-permissions).
</Note>

## What it captures

The log is paginated and filterable by action type, so you can narrow to a
specific kind of event: sign-ins, role changes, integration changes, and other
configuration activity, during a review or investigation.

<Tip>
  Pair the audit log with [SSO enforcement](/access/okta-saml-onboarding) and
  [SCIM](/access/okta-saml-onboarding) so access changes are both governed and
  recorded.
</Tip>
