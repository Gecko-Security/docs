Manage your subscription in **Settings** &gt; **Billing**, and compare plans in
**Settings** &gt; **Plans**.

## Plans

| Plan | Best for |
| --- | --- |
| Free | Evaluating Gecko and scanning a few repositories. |
| Pro | Teams that want notifications, API access, and ongoing scanning. |
| Enterprise | Organizations needing SSO/SCIM, audit logs, and hybrid deployment. |

## Usage

The Billing page shows your current plan and status, scan usage (used, limit,
and remaining), and member count, so you can see where you stand against your
plan's limits.

## Feature availability

Some capabilities are plan-gated:

- **[Slack](/slack-integration)**: Pro and above.
- **[API keys](/admin/api-keys)**: Pro and Enterprise.
- **[SSO / SCIM](/access/okta-saml-onboarding)**, **[audit log](/admin/audit-log)**,
  and **[hybrid deployment](/deployment/architecture)**: Enterprise.

<Tip>
  Need a higher limit or an Enterprise feature? Reach out via the **Plans** page
  or contact your Gecko account team.
</Tip>
