Connect ClickUp to track remediation alongside your team's existing work.
Configure it in **Settings** &gt; **ClickUp**.

## Connect

<Steps>
<Step title="Authorize ClickUp">
  Click **Connect** and complete the ClickUp OAuth flow.
</Step>
<Step title="Choose a destination">
  Select a **Space**, then a **List** within that space where tasks should be
  created.
</Step>
<Step title="Enable auto-create">
  Turn on **auto-create** to file a ClickUp task for every finding after each
  scan.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Point Gecko at a dedicated security list so findings don't get lost in a busy
  backlog.
</Tip>
