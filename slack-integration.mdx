Connect Slack so the right team sees security work without living inside Gecko.
Configure it in **Settings** &gt; **Slack**.

<Note>
  The Slack integration requires the Pro plan.
</Note>

## Connect

<Steps>
<Step title="Install the Gecko Slack app">
  Click **Connect** and approve the Slack app install for your workspace.
</Step>
<Step title="Route channels to repositories">
  Map one or more Slack channels to **All repositories** or to specific repos.
  Add as many routes as you need. For private channels, invite the bot with
  `/invite @Gecko Security`.
</Step>
<Step title="Filter the noise">
  Set a **minimum severity** (Critical, High, Medium, or Low) and choose whether
  to notify on [PR scans](/scanning/pr-checks) and
  [scheduled scans](/scanning/scheduled-scans).
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Route Critical and High findings to a focused security channel, and send
  lower-severity or scheduled-scan updates to a quieter channel.
</Tip>
