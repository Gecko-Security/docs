## What to expect from Gecko

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Fast setup" icon="rocket">
    Most teams connect a code host, choose a repository, and launch a first
    scan in minutes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Actionable evidence" icon="search-check">
    Every finding should give you code context, explanation, and enough detail
    to decide whether to fix now, track, or dismiss.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Continuous workflow" icon="git-branch">
    Gecko works best when you rerun scans after fixes, keep integrations
    connected, and treat security reviews as part of normal delivery.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
