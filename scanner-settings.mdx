---
title: "Scanner settings"
description: "Configure scan behavior, schedules, and rules."
---

Use **Settings** to control how Gecko scans your repositories.

| Setting | Where | When to configure |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Repository selection | Scan flow | When adding new repos to scan |
| Scan cadence | Schedules | After your baseline workflow is stable |
| Rules | Rules | When you want to tune which findings surface |
| Integrations | Integrations | When findings are ready to move outside Gecko |
